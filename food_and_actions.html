Food and Actions

Cucumber: Clears heat and detoxifies the body, reducing inflammation.

Watermelon: Relieves heat and generates fluids, cooling the body.

Mung Beans: Detoxifies and clears heat, often used in soups for cooling and detoxification.

Ginger: Promotes circulation and digestion, treating colds and improving warmth.

Garlic: Stimulates digestion and enhances immunity, expelling cold and dampness.

Cinnamon: Warms the body and enhances circulation, used in teas to promote warmth.

Kidney Yin: Black beans, duck, and seaweed nourish and hydrate, restoring kidney yin.

Spleen Qi: Sweet potatoes, dates, and oats strengthen the spleen, improving digestion and energy.

Liver Qi: Green leafy vegetables, celery, and beets promote smooth flow of liver qi and detoxification.
