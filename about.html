About Chinese Medicine

Chinese Medicine is an ancient system of health and wellness that's been used in China for thousands of years. Practitioners use herbs, diet, acupuncture, cupping, and qigong to treat or prevent health problems. The principles of Chinese Medicine are based on the concepts of Qi (vital energy), Yin and Yang (balance), and the Five Elements (Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water).

These methods aim to restore balance and harmony within the body, ensuring the smooth flow of Qi. Acupuncture, for instance, involves the insertion of fine needles into specific points on the body to unblock or redirect Qi, promoting healing and well-being.
